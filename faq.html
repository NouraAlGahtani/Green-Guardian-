Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water my plant?

Most plants need watering when the top inch of soil feels dry. However, the frequency can vary based on plant type and environment.

What kind of sunlight does my plant need?

Different plants have different sunlight needs. While some thrive in direct sunlight, others prefer indirect or shaded light.
